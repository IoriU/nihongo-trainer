# 📕 にほんご ノート — My Japanese Course

A personal textbook built one lesson at a time, paced to my progress.

> **Learner:** Native Indonesian 🇮🇩 · reads hiragana · learning katakana
> **Goal:** 🏯 Living / studying in Japan
> **Pace:** ~45–60 min/day · one lesson at a time

---

## How to use this book

1. **Read** the lesson top to bottom — don't skip the culture notes.
2. **Say everything out loud.** Reading silently builds zero speaking ability.
3. **Do the exercises** at the bottom, then check the answer key.
4. **Add new vocab to Anki** (or a notebook) the same day.
5. When you can do the exercises without looking back → tell me **"ready for the next lesson"** and I'll write it.

Each lesson has the same shape so it feels familiar:
`🎯 Goals · 🗣️ Vocabulary · 🧩 Grammar · 💬 Dialogue · 🏯 Culture · ✍️ Exercises · ✅ Answers · 📦 Vocab list`

---

## 🎮 Practice app

Open **[app/index.html](app/index.html)** in Edge or Chrome (or the hosted PWA) for an interactive trainer:
- **Learn** — read each lesson right inside the app (the `app/lessons/*.md` files)
- **Kana** — katakana/hiragana drills (type or multiple-choice) with audio
- **Flashcards** — spaced-repetition review, auto-loaded with each lesson's vocab
- **Stats** — streak, accuracy, cards due, and cross-device sync

Progress saves in your browser (and syncs across devices on the hosted site).
